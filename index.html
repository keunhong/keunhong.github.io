Hello, I'm Keunhong Park

I am a Ph. D student at the University of Washington with Ali Farhadi and Steve Seitz.

I work at the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics. I have worked on automatically translating 2D representations (images) into more dynamic representations (geometry and appearance). I am currently exploring ways to leverage multi-modal data for scene understanding.
